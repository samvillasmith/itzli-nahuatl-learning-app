# Notices and Attributions

## Nāhuatlahtolli Source Course

Itzli includes adapted lesson text and extracted vocabulary from
**Nāhuatlahtolli: A Beginner to Advanced Level Nahuatl Online Course**.

- Original course: https://tlahtolli.coerll.utexas.edu/
- OER listing: https://coerll.utexas.edu/coerll/oer/nahuatlahtolli/
- Publisher: COERLL, The University of Texas at Austin
- Authors: Sabina de la Cruz, Catalina de la Cruz, Josefrayn Sánchez-Perry,
  Kelly McDonough, and Sergio Romero
- License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International
  (CC BY-SA 4.0), https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/

Changes made for Itzli include public-page extraction, text cleanup, section
formatting, source-link preservation, and integration into the Itzli unit and
source-course interfaces. Media files are retained as links to the original
source URLs; this repository does not download or rehost the course media.

UT Austin, COERLL, AILLA, and LLILAS logos and marks are not imported as Itzli
app assets.
